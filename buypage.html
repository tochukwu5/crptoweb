Getting Started

  • Setting up a wallet

    • Step 1: Download Metamask Wallet (skip to step 5 if you already have metamask installed)
    • Step 2: Install and open the wallet on your device.
    • Step 3: Create a new wallet or import an existing one.
    • Step 4: Set up a password and secure your wallet.
    • Step 5: Add Egochain Network to your metamask using the link: Metamask https://chainlist.org/chain/5439

  • Bridging to Egochain

    • Option 1: Buy $EGAX from Mexc and withdraw directly from Mexc to your Metamask Wallet. Ensure that your wallet address is correct to avoid any issues.
    • Option 2: Use the Official Egoswap Bridge . Follow the prompt instructions to bridge your assets to Egochain from another network.
